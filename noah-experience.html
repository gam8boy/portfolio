My Internship at NOAH Business Applications

A narrative overview of my OJT (On-the-Job Training) journey.

Company Background

NOAH Business Applications is an IT company offering smart business solutions. My internship there exposed me to real-world processes in development, QA testing, and documentation.

Internship Goals

  • Apply classroom knowledge to actual work scenarios
  • Contribute to real system testing and documentation
  • Observe how development teams manage tasks and timelines

What I Worked On

During my time at NOAH, I worked with the QA and Dev team for their BRTS (Business Rules Testing System) and BRSS (Business Rules Support System). I was in charge of:

  • Documenting test results
  • Capturing screenshots for QA reporting
  • Improving the user experience by identifying errors
  • Collaborating with developers to report issues

Challenges and Learnings

One key challenge was learning to spot edge-case bugs during testing and understanding how user-centered design plays a role even in internal tools. I also improved my attention to detail and communication skills while documenting bugs for the dev team.
